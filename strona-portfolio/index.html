Hi. My name is Michał, I'm 25 years old. I became interested in programming after I graduated from high school. I've always had a lot of fun writing code, and the moment you solve a problem you've been sitting on all night is one of the most beautiful experiences you can have as a programmer. With time, I decided to develop my skills a bit, so I took the Front-End-Developer course at the Code Academy in Warsaw. The skills that I gained there allowed me to start creating websites, for myself, but also for commercial use. I decided to continue my education, choosing to study closely related to Programming, in the meantime I also started a business, where I write websites and learn to play the drums.
